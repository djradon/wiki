Metaverse Vs Multiverse

Metaverse:

An interconnected digital world where users can interact, socialize, and create. Often associated with VR, AR, and digital economies. It’s essentially shared digital spaces.

Multiverse (cultural)

A conceptual collection of all our created realities, including ones created in the Metaverse, literature, film, and video games. Each universe within the multiverse can have different timelines.

In short, the metaverse is about digital experiences, while the multiverse is a collection of cultural and literary realities.
