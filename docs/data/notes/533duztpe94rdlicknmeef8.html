Information Bearing Entity

refers to any physical entity that can carry or store information. IBEs are the physical carriers of ICEs. They provide the medium through which information content can be stored, transmitted, or retrieved. Examples:

  • A physical book (the paper and ink that carry the text)
  • A USB drive (the hardware storing digital data)
  • A CD-ROM (the physical disc containing digital content)
