Relator Compound

  • formerly-known-as: relator cluster, relator-atom
  • a form of Relationship to Entity Conversion composed of a Relator and its composing statements
  • similar to an Ontogen triple compound, except can't group multiple unrelated compounded-triples
    • i.e., compounds are mostly about attribution
